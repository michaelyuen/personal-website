In a time where life increasingly digital, gratification is expected to be instant, and human interaction has fallen by the wayside, I try to approach everything with passion, patience and perspective so as to reclaim the beauty of these forgotten values.

As Alan Moore eloquently stated, everybody has their story to tell. Unfortunately, more often than not these stories lose their value to all but the teller.

I don't like that

It's easy to hear; harder to listen. I try to listen to everybody's stories as if they were my own and appreciate what they mean to the person brave enough to tell them. If you have a story you'd like to tell or a project you'd like to build, contact me.
